Bird Definitions

Black Crowned Crane
A majestic bird known for its striking golden crest and graceful movements.
Perched Bird
A beautiful small bird often found sitting on tree branches, known for its chirping.
Avian Bird
A general term for birds in flight, showcasing their elegant wingspan.
Grey Heron
A tall wading bird commonly seen near water bodies, known for its long beak and patience in hunting.
